About Me

My professional journey and personal philosophy

Krishna Bhakta Bramhacharya

With over three decades of distinguished service in security and law enforcement, I have dedicated my career to protecting communities, building secure environments, and fostering international cooperation through peacekeeping missions.

My journey began in the Nepal Army, where I developed foundational skills in security operations and leadership. After transitioning to the Armed Police Force (APF) Nepal, I rose through the ranks to become Deputy Inspector General of Police (DIGP), overseeing critical security operations and policy implementations.

My international experience includes serving in United Nations peacekeeping missions, where I coordinated multinational teams in complex security environments. These experiences have given me unique insights into global security challenges and cross-cultural leadership.

Today, I leverage this extensive experience to provide strategic security consulting, training programs, and thought leadership to organizations facing complex security challenges.

Professional Experience Overview

My career spans over three decades in security and law enforcement, with progressive responsibilities from field operations to executive leadership:

  • United Nations Peacekeeping Officer - Various missions (2006-2009)

Key responsibilities included strategic security planning, crisis management, intelligence coordination, and multinational team leadership.

  • Implemented security protocols for civilian protection in conflict zones
